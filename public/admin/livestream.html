📋 How to go live:
  1. Start your stream on YouTube, Facebook, or your platform of choice
  2. Copy the stream/watch URL once it's live
  3. Paste it in the URL field above and click Go Live
  4. Members will see a live indicator and can join from their dashboard
  5. When finished, click End Stream to take it offline
