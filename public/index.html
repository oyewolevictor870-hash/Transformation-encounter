Our Story

Transformation Encounter Group began as a WhatsApp prayer group — a simple gathering of believers passionate about God. What started small has grown into a full ministry, because when fire burns in hearts, it cannot be contained.

We are a community built on the foundation of prayer, the Word of God, and accountability. Every member is on a journey — and we journey together.

🎯 Vision: Men on Fire for God. Revival Fire Everywhere. Restoration and Revival.
🚀 Mission: Transform Lives. Touch Lives Positively. Awaken Sleeping Saints.
